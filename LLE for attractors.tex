	The Lorenz system:
	\begin{displaymath}
		\left\{\begin{array}{l}
		\dot{x} = -\sigma x + \sigma y\\
		\dot{y} = \gamma x - y -xz \\
		\dot{z} = xy - bz
		\end{array}
		\right.
	\end{displaymath}
	
	Jacobian:
	\begin{displaymath}
		\mathbf{J}=
		\left(\begin{array}{ccc}
		-\sigma & \sigma & 0 \\
		\gamma-z & -1 & -x \\
		y & x & -b 
		\end{array}
		\right)
	\end{displaymath}
